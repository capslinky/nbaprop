This file explains all columns in the daily report CSV.

================================================================================
SECTION 1: IDENTIFIERS
================================================================================

report_date         Date of the analysis (MM-DD-YYYY)
player              Player's full name
player_team         3-letter abbreviation of player's team (e.g., LAL, BOS)
opponent            3-letter abbreviation of opposing team
home_away           HOME = playing at home, AWAY = playing on the road
matchup             Full matchup format: "AWAY @ HOME"

================================================================================
SECTION 2: PROP INFORMATION
================================================================================

prop_type           Type of prop bet:
                    - points: Total points scored
                    - rebounds: Total rebounds
                    - assists: Total assists
                    - pra: Points + Rebounds + Assists combined
                    - threes: 3-point field goals made

line                The betting line (e.g., 25.5 means over/under 25.5)
over_odds           American odds for the OVER (e.g., -110, +120)
bookmaker           Sportsbook offering this line (draftkings, fanduel, etc.)

================================================================================
SECTION 3: RAW PLAYER STATISTICS (Current 2025-26 Season)
================================================================================

season_avg          Average for this stat across all games this season
recent_5_avg        Average for the last 5 games only (recent form)
median              Median value (middle value, less affected by outliers)
std_dev             Standard deviation (higher = more volatile/inconsistent)
min                 Lowest value recorded this season
max                 Highest value recorded this season
games_analyzed      Number of games used in analysis (up to 15)
times_over          How many games the player went OVER this line
times_under         How many games the player went UNDER this line
times_push          How many games the player hit exactly on the line
over_rate_pct       Percentage of games player went over (times_over/games Ã— 100)

================================================================================
SECTION 4: CONTEXT FACTORS
================================================================================

is_back_to_back     TRUE if playing second game in 2 days (rest disadvantage)
days_rest           Days since last game (1 = back-to-back, 2+ = rested)

recent_mins_avg     Average minutes in last 5 games
season_mins_avg     Average minutes for full season
minutes_factor      Recent mins / Season mins:
                    - > 1.0 = playing MORE minutes recently
                    - < 1.0 = playing FEWER minutes recently

opp_defense_factor  Opponent's defensive factor for this stat type:
                    - > 1.0 = opponent ALLOWS more than average (good matchup)
                    - < 1.0 = opponent ALLOWS less than average (bad matchup)
                    - 1.05 = opponent allows 5% more than league average

opp_defense_rank    Opponent's rank in allowing this stat (1-30):
                    - 1-5 = Worst defense (allows most) = SMASH SPOT
                    - 6-10 = Below average defense = Good matchup
                    - 11-20 = Average defense
                    - 21-25 = Above average defense
                    - 26-30 = Best defense (allows least) = TOUGH matchup

team_pace_factor    Player's team pace factor:
                    - > 1.0 = faster than average = more possessions
                    - < 1.0 = slower than average = fewer possessions

opp_pace_factor     Opponent's pace factor

combined_pace       Average of both teams' pace factors

vegas_game_total    Vegas over/under for total game points (e.g., 225.5)
                    - High (235+) = expected shootout = boost scoring props
                    - Low (215-) = expected defensive game = reduce scoring props

blowout_risk        Risk of blowout based on spread:
                    - HIGH = spread > 10 points (starters may rest)
                    - MEDIUM = spread 7-10 points
                    - LOW = spread < 7 points (competitive game)

base_projection     Initial projection before context adjustments

confidence_pct      Model confidence (0-100%):
                    - Based on consistency, sample size, trend strength
                    - Higher = more reliable projection

recommended_pick    Model recommendation:
                    - OVER = bet the over
                    - UNDER = bet the under  
                    - PASS = edge too small or confidence too low
